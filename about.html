ABOUT WHAT WE DO

WE ARE DEDICATED TO CREATING THE BEST RESULTS IN THE SHORTEST TIME.

GumBusters is a surface cleaning company specializing in removing tough stains such as chewing gum, paint, mould, and general grime from various surfaces using high-pressure hot water and steam. With 15 years of experience, we ensure thorough cleaning without damaging concrete. Our expertise lies in removing gum from hard surfaces without causing damage. Unlike other methods that can gouge concrete, our techniques guarantee effective gum removal while preserving surface integrity.

We take pride in our ability to deliver exceptional results while maintaining the quality of your surfaces. GumBusters offers comprehensive stain removal services, from gum and graffiti to general grime. Using customized techniques, we ensure spotless results, leaving your surfaces looking as good as new. Contact us today to restore the cleanliness and appearance of your property.
