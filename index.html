EXPERTS IN CONCRETE STAIN REMOVAL

GumBusters specializes in removing tough stains such as chewing gum, paint, mould, and general grime from various surfaces using high-pressure hot water and steam. With 15 years of experience, we ensure thorough cleaning without damaging concrete. Our team of skilled technicians is equipped with the latest technology and eco-friendly cleaning solutions to tackle even the most stubborn stains, restoring your surfaces to their original condition.

NO OUTDOOR SURFACE IS TOO DIRTY

Our expertise lies in removing gum from hard surfaces without causing damage. Unlike high-pressure water that can gouge concrete, our methods guarantee effective gum removal without compromising the surface integrity. Whether it's sidewalks, driveways, or building exteriors, no outdoor surface is too dirty for GumBusters to handle. We take pride in our ability to deliver exceptional results while preserving the integrity of your concrete surfaces.

OUR REMOVAL SERVICES

GumBusters offers comprehensive stain removal services. From gum and graffiti to general grime, we use customized techniques to ensure spotless results. Our team of experts is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and years of experience to tackle even the toughest stains, leaving your surfaces looking as good as new.

Chewing Gum

Our specialized techniques effectively remove chewing gum residues without damaging the surface. Whether it's stuck on sidewalks, pavements, or other hard surfaces, we can get rid of gum quickly and efficiently, leaving your surfaces looking pristine.

Paint

We expertly eliminate paint stains from various surfaces, restoring their original appearance. Whether it's accidental spills or graffiti, our team knows how to safely and effectively remove paint without causing damage to the underlying surface.

Mould & General Grime

Our methods are tailored to safely remove mould and general grime, ensuring a clean and hygienic environment. Using eco-friendly cleaning solutions and specialized equipment, we can eliminate mould and grime buildup.
